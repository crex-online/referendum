Referendum

A group decision-making and polling platform

Direct democracy is the future, but how might it work in practice?

How can increasingly larger groups of people come to a consensus, when there are too many of them to fit into one room?

Referendum is a simple platform, created out of a desire to answer those questions. I wanted for people to be able to make dynamic polls, where voters could contribute the options themselves. I wanted the system to feature discussion before voting, a key feature in collaborative systems. I wanted it to have bottom-up democracy, minimizing voter regret by using the best voting method possible, Range Voting (also known as olympic score voting).

Check out a sample poll here.

  • Threaded discussions and polls, using Range Voting.
